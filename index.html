About Plumbing Fault Finder

Built by a qualified UK plumber and heating engineer to give homeowners and tradespeople a fast, honest, jargon-free resource for diagnosing plumbing and heating problems.

Why we built this

Every day as a working plumber, the same questions come up time and time again. Homeowners calling in a panic about a fault code on their boiler that simply means the pressure has dropped. People paying emergency call-out fees for problems they could have fixed themselves in ten minutes.

Plumbing Fault Finder exists to change that. We believe clear, accurate information — written by someone who actually does this work every day — can save people time, money, and stress.

Who is this for?

🏠

Homeowners

Diagnose what's wrong before calling a plumber. Understand the problem and whether it's safe to wait or urgent.

🏢

Landlords

Diagnose tenant-reported issues remotely and understand whether a job needs an emergency call-out.

🔧

Tradespeople

Our Pro version gives Gas Safe engineers full technical fault guides, component diagnostics, and printable job sheets — launching soon.

A note on gas safety

⚠️ Gas work is legally restricted in the UK. Only Gas Safe registered engineers are permitted to work on gas appliances, boilers, and gas pipework. We never provide DIY fix instructions for gas work. Verify any engineer at gassaferegister.co.uk.

Our Pro version

We're launching a Pro subscription for Gas Safe engineers at £4.99/month — full technical repair guides, component diagnostics, printable job sheets, pipe sizing calculators, and the ability to list your business on the site.
